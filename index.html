About me

My name is Sylwia Guzenda and I am a makeup enthusiast and professional !
Beautifying is my great passion and a love of mine. I've been interested in makeup for as long as I can remember. This passion for makeup in my personal life led me to making this my profession; so I could bring this love and drive to my chosen career. Professionally applied makeup takes time and consideration, and so I approach it systematically and individually. I carefully bespoke classic makeup techniques to suit the individual. I have completed workshops with makeup artists such as Magda Pieczonka. I am comfortable working in various settings including, but not limited to, weddings and photo shoots. I am currently based in Windsor, Berkshire. I do what I love and I aim to give all my clients the glow of confidence that comes from great makeup, and feeling wonderful about how they look.
